Electron

The powerful hero Electron is the hero who started the VH universe of heroes. He is a college student by the name of Nathan Kempt who got his powers by an experiment that changes the nature of the electrons in the air from negative to positive on command. After being shocked by the experiment that instilled him with the power to change the charge of electrons to either create powerful bolts of lightning or to slow down the elements in the air in the field of the positive electrons. This hero is sometimes referred to as the "jumper" because of the leaps Electron can muster.
